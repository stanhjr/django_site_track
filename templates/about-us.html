About us

The employees of buymyfleet have a combined experience of 125 years in the buying and selling of commercial trucks and trailers. We have great experience in the marketing of commercial vehicles. Let is put our expertise to work for you in the disposal of your used equipment.

Why we started this site. We have noticed for years that there is a need for a cost effective way to help small to midsized fleet liquidate there used equipment.

Why and auction site? The used equipment market seems to change daily and price fluctuations can at times be huge. In different markets there are different prices , some higher than in your area and some lower. Let us market your trucks to a Nationwide audience to help you get the best value for your equipment

Why buymyfleet auction compared to the others ? Momentarily we are offering a no cost to all sellers of equipment , that right FREE to sellers while buyers will pay only a 150$ per month fee to purchase as many trucks as they choose. There are no buyers fees , no seller fees and no percentages.

Marketing , we will market your equipment to thousands of buyers across the USA. The seller will have a choice of who he wants to market the trucks to. To dealers only or to everyone that wants to buy. We will use all available tools to market your equipment to the right buyers
